Crescent Dogs

Ingredients

Original recipe makes 8 sandwiches

  • 8 hot dogs
  • 4 slices American cheese, each cut into 6 strips
  • 1 (8 ounce) can Pillsbury® refrigerated crescent dinner rolls

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Slit hot dogs to within 1/2 inch of ends; insert 3 strips of cheese into each slit.
  3. Separate dough into triangles.
  4. Wrap dough triangle around each hot dog.
  5. Place on ungreased cookie sheet, cheese side up.
  6. Bake 12-15 min or until golden brown.
